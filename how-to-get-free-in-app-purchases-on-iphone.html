How to jailbreak iOS 13.5 and below

How to get free In-App-Purchases on supported apps

For this tutorial you will need a Jailbroken iDevice, if you are not yet Jailbroken, follow my guide above.

-

This method allows you to press the "Cancel" button instead of going through with an IAP, and you will still recieve what the app "thinks" is being bought.

-

Open the Cydia app on your device and add this source. https://ipgrab.github.io/repo or click here.

-

Open the source that was just added and install the package called "InApp Purchases iOS 10.x" even though it says iOS 10 it still works on iOS 13.5.

-

Disclaimer: This method does not work on every app and wont work on games by Supercell (Clash of Clans.) A game that does work for example would be DragonVale.

-

MAKE SURE YOU DO NOT USE TOUCHID/FACEID WHEN ON IN APP PURCHASE SCREEN, ONLY EVER PRESS "CANCEL"
