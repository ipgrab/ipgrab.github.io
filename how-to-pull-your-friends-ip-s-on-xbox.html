Pull Your Friends IP’s on Xbox Live in 10 Steps!

  • Home

First off, you’re going to have a Windows Computer, if you do not have one, that sucks.

Second, you will need to download LANC and WinPcap from the button like right here!

 

Third, open WinPcap_4_1_3.exe to run the installer. (You are now done with WinPcap.)

Fourth, open the LANC folder and run LANC.exe

Fifth, once you have LANC.exe opened, look where it says “Network Adapter” and choose which ever one works. A way you can tell if it works is if it allows you to check “ARPSpoof” and “Filter”, if an error pops up, choose a different selection until one works.

Sixth, set the “Source Port” and “Destination Port” to 3074. (Sometimes, the port will need to be set to 3075, but that’s rare.)

Seventh, you will need to head over to your Xbox One, and join someones Xbox Live party.

Eighth, on your Xbox One, head over to Settings press the Network tab, then press Network Settings.

Ninth, press on Advanced settings and under “IPv4” write down or remember the IP address.

Tenth, head back over to your computer and in LANC, type in your Xbox’s IP address in “To:”. The “From:” tab should be your routers IP, which should already be detected by LANC.

Press the “Begin” Button!

(The IP addresses shown in the Connections tab are your friends IP addresses.)
